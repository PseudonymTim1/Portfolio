Unusual

About:
Unusual is a quirky, weird, action turn based RPG, with a unique and fun in-depth battle system combining together keymatching rhythm game combos, and first person shooter combat. Play as an optimistic feline in a nonsensical crazy world not bound by the confines of logic, recruiting unique party members of various skills to assist you on your pointless quest!
