Doom 1 Esque RPG

About:
I decided to experiment with creating a Doom-like looter shooter game a while back, as I love sprite based FPS's. For this small project I ripped a few Doom and doom mod assets, wrote up some simple copycat doom 1 imp AI, doom sprite rendering, a doom-like FPS controller, and an in-depth customizable weapon system. I had planned to also write up some procedural weapon generation and other RPG stuff (like Borderlands) but I just kinda didn't work on it one day so it faded out of my mind. Although it's abandoned I may come back to it in the future as the idea of a game like this seems fun, or at least it'd be useful for basing another project in the future so I might come back to it but it'll most likely be used for another purpose with some stuff rewritten.
