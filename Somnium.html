Somnium

About:
Somnium is a psychological survival horror esque retro horror game being developed mostly by members from the Whackjob interactive devteam, the guys behind Catharsis Reborn. Somnium aims to provide a unique combination of horror experiences that you won't find a mix of anywhere else, featuring unsettling sound design systems, randomized hallucination scares, and generally mechanics that constantly keep you on your toes and wondering what could possibly happen next, being inspired by the Penumbra series of games. Play as a Insomnia stricken private investigator assigned a mysterious case and venture through the otherworldly realm in your sleep to accomplish your mission and leave with your life.
