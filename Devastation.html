Dev-astation

About:
Dev-astation is a puzzle platformer in which you play as Toaster cat, a toast offering robot made but tossed aside by it's creator as a stupid idea. Shorting awake from a leaky ceiling with no memory, utilize your in-game developer console to interact with the world and solve puzzles to find out your true purpose and escape!
