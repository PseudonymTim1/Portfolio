Dead End

About:
A "time-based-interactathon" platformer made for The Weekly Game Jam #130. Run around and find cool objects to interact with! Earn points and time bonuses every time you interact with them! Die to live again! Compete with people on an online leaderboard for the highest score! Why? I don't know! But it's kinda fun! You are a totally blessed highly intelligent being, don't squander your totally meaningful existence as a corporeal entity of this sacred realm and dimension!
