Project Decimation

About:
An ultraviolent RPG FPS where you play as Charlie, a human brought back to life from death via a prototype endoskeleton, converted by a opposing Rebel faction after being turned into a Decimaton, a litteral killing machine. Fight for your life and make great use of your new being as you slash and shoot your way through the post apocolyptic remains of New york to get your revenge against a despicable man who decimated multiple dimensions, and who is toying with your world.
