Husk

About:
HUSK was made for the 2020 Haunted PS1 Game Jam in a week and was pretty much finished but I decided that I wasn't happy to release it in the state it was in and the time I had left in the deadline (had some complications with my level designer noping out so I had to do all the levels myself which I initially did NOT want to have to worry about), so I shelved it intending to polish it off later. Maybe I'll return to it someday. I mainly did this project to really test my art skills and I learned a lot from it so I guess mission accomplished. And yes, this game was inspired by Dusk, how could you tell. You play as a deranged psychopath of a cult investigating a strange farming village in the middle of the woods in the late 1800's after your village has been cut off from supplies. You go around the village possessing enemies to gain their abilities to solve puzzles and help you fight the possessed and mutilated village people.
