EXCAVATOR

About:
EXCAVATOR is a game where you play the last mining bot of a large corporation in the year 2056, sent on a suicide mission deep underground to recover resources long since buried and lost to time, as resources above earth are completely ran dry due to numerous apocolyptic events. Explore, mine, terraform, loot, and fight your way through procedurally generated cave systems and structures as you meet the demands of your desperate human overlords... or die trying
