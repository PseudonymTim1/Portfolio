Dredger

About:
Dredger is a rougelite old school dungeon-crawler, comparable to the binding of issac. Run around semi-randomized dungeons, find cool trinkets to give you neat new abilities, find secrets, kill some whacky enemies, and find the keys to progress to the next dungeon to escape with your newly found riches! All while listening to random tunes that change the pacing of the game to shake things up and completing wario-ware esque minigames to perform actions!
