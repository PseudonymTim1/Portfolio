Tetrad

About:
Tetrad is a "multi-dimensional shooter platformer" where you play as a multi-dimensional assassin, having the ability to control up to four versions of yourself independently in four different alternate versions of the level you are on. Solve puzzles to affect things other dimensions, interact between them by shooting interdimensionally, as well as throwing your other self a gun! Don't allow one of the versions of yourself to die before they all reach the end of the level or assassinated their target or all of you will die!
