Robo-Romp (GMTK)

About:
Robo romp is a top down 2D shoot em up where instead of a traditional health system, your player looses control of their player gradually, key by key. Fight through waves of randomized robotic combatants for a highscore! Dash to avoid enemies, upgrade your stats, and buy new weapons! But be careful though, it's very easy to loose control! Made for the 2020 GMTK game jam in 48 hours.
